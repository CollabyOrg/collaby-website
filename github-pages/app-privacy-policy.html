Collaby Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Collaby, a social media app that matches content creators who make similar content to collaborate. We take user privacy seriously and have created this privacy policy to explain how we collect, use, and protect user data. This policy is in compliance with global privacy standards and regulations, including those of Colombia, Mexico, and Argentina. By using our app, you agree to this policy.

Data collected

Collaby collects the following data from users:

  • The device language specified in the OS settings.
  • The user authentication data: Only the user's email and password. This data is managed and stored by AWS Cognito, not by us or on our servers.
  • The location of the user as a content creator, specified during account creation. This location includes the country, the territorial subdivision, and the city.
  • The language spoken by the content creator in all of their content.
  • The linked platform stats related to the account of the user, such as the number of followers, the average total number of views, the total number of likes, comments, and dislikes for all posts, and the total number of times someone has shared one of their posts. Platforms include Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and Twitch. We only obtain data from platforms that users link to their account, and only with user authorization following the OAuth 2.0 protocol.
  • Platform account data, such as channel name and Instagram username.
  • Creator tags related to the content the creator makes, obtained from a field during account creation.
  • A video provided by each user where they present the type of content they create.

Data use

Collaby uses the collected data as follows:

  • To match users with content creators who make similar content. This data is used for filtering and sorting search results.
  • To present linked platform stats in a clear and easy-to-understand data visualization to every user, only within the app. Users who create a Collaby account agree that their platform stats will be publicly displayed to other users who also use Collaby.
  • To display all text in the app in the language the user has chosen in their OS settings.
  • To display the video provided by each user when other users are searching for creators to collaborate with.

Data sharing

Collaby only shares collected data with AWS, our default service provider. We do not share data with all AWS services, only with AWS Cognito, where we store user authentication data, AWS DynamoDB, where we store alphanumeric data other than user authentication data, and AWS S3, where we store multimedia data and assets such as the video provided by each user.

Data protection

Collaby takes data protection seriously and offers the following measures:

  • Option to use TOTP and 2FA with an authenticator app, such as Google Authenticator, in addition to a password to access Collaby.
  • Regularly updated and maintained servers to ensure data security.
  • All data transmission is encrypted.

Conclusion

Collaby is committed to protecting user privacy and complying with global privacy regulations. By using our app, you agree to our privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@collaby.com
